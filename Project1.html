Project Description

A Technical Writing project, where I was to describe all the steps necessary to perform the changing of a tire tube on a bicycle. These instructions were designed for beginners and those who may not be knowledgable on all the technical terms bicycles may have. In order to achieve this, I used a combination of easy to understand instructions and images to help guide readers.

Skills Learned

  • Analyzing the intended audience and their levels of understanding
  • Creating steps in a way that is easy to digest
  • How to make technical definitions regarding technical processes
  • How to make documents consistent and easy to read for the audience
